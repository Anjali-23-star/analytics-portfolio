## Objective
Identifying the driving factor behind revenue concentration.


## Dataset
Online retail dataset(Kaggle) containing Invoice No, Stock code, Description, Quantity, Invoice Date, Unit price, Customer ID, country.


## Key Questions
1. Do high revenue customers purchase more frequently?
2. If not, what is the factor that is driving the revenue?


## Approach
- Analyse the month to month purchase frequency of the customer.
- Analyse the order value for the customer.


## Key findings
- Customer generating the higest revenue are not frequent purchasers.
- These customers however have high value transactions.
- Price point analysis is very important in any business.


## Business Implications
- The purchase frequency and revenue are not always correlated.
- The high revenue generating customers need different marketing strategies to retain them.
- The business is much more sensitive to them as compared to the more frequent customers who brings comparatively less revenue.
